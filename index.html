The legend of Peixe Boi

This is the start

Once upon a time, Christopher Columbus was sailing the ocean blue.

And he saw something he thought was a mermaid.

Turns out it was a manatee, an animal the Portuguese would later call "o peixe boi".

You may be saying, what? O peixi boi looks NOTHING like a mermaid! What was Columbus thinking?

But let me remind you, this doofus thought the American continent was India.

Let me give you five reasons to hate Columbus while I'm at it:

  1. He was racist
  2. He was stupid
  3. He was a murderer
  4. He thought America was India
  5. He didn't even discover America. Loser.
